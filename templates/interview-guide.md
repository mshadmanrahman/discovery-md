## Before You Start

### Mindset Check
- You are here to **learn**, not to **sell** or **validate your idea**
- You want to hear "this isn't a problem for me" as much as "yes, this is painful"
- If every interview confirms your hypothesis, you're probably asking leading questions

### Logistics
- **Duration:** 45 minutes conversation + 15 minutes buffer
- **Recording:** Ask permission. "Do you mind if I record this? It helps me focus on listening instead of note-taking."
- **Note-taker:** Bring one if possible. If not, record and review.

## Bias Checklist

Review before each interview:

- [ ] **Confirmation bias:** I believe {assumption}. I will actively listen for evidence AGAINST it.
- [ ] **Leading questions:** I will not mention my solution or feature idea unless asked.
- [ ] **Recency bias:** I won't weight the last interview more than earlier ones.
- [ ] **Selection bias:** My interviewee list includes {diversity check: role, company size, geography, usage level}.
- [ ] **Courtesy bias:** If they say something positive, I'll probe: "Can you tell me about a time that DIDN'T work?"
